Time line of B.J. Habibie's life


  • 1936 - Born in Parepare, South Sulawesi, Indonesia to husband and wife Alwi Abdul Jalil Habibie and R. A. Tuti Marini Puspowardojo.

  • 1954 - Habibie studied mechanical engineering at the Faculty of Engineering, University of Indonesia Bandung (now the Bandung Institute of Technology).

  • 1960s - Develops the "Crack Propagation Theory" while studying in Germany, earning him the nickname "Mr. Crack."

  • 1978s-1998s - Bj Habibie served as Minister of State for Research and Technology in the Development Cabinet.

  • 1998 - Becomes Vice President of Indonesia.

  • 1998 - Assumes the presidency after Soeharto's resignation.

  • 1999 - Steps down after a successful transition period leading to democratic elections.

  • 2019 - Passes away at the age of 83, leaving a lasting legacy in Indonesian aviation and technology.
